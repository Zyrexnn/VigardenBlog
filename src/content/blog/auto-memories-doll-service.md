## What is an Auto Memories Doll

In the post-war world, many people carried feelings they could not put into words. Things they wanted to tell their loved ones, but did not know how to express.

For such people, the Auto Memories Doll Service was born.

### Our Work

The work of an Auto Memories Doll is simple. But it is precisely because it is simple that it is difficult.

**We listen to the client's voice.** Not just the words on the surface, but the true feelings hidden beneath them.

**We choose the words.** One by one, carefully, the words most fitting for the client's heart.

**We write the letter.** With ink and pen, we engrave the client's heart onto paper.

### The Sound of Typewriters

In the CH Postal Company office, the sound of typewriters always echoes. Click-clack-click-clack. It is like the heartbeat of us Auto Memories Dolls.

Every letter, filled with feeling.

> "If your heart so desires, we will gladly come to assist you anywhere."

### Dolls for Hire

We go anywhere in the country. To a small village deep in the mountains, to a foreign land across the sea.

Entrust the words of your heart to us. We will deliver them, without fail.
