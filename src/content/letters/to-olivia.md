Olivia,

Papa wanted to go to that lake one more time. The lake where we used to play when you were still well.

You opened your umbrella and said, "Papa, look! I can fly!" And truly, you flew on the wind. With that tiny umbrella, just like a fairy.

Papa is a storyteller. But since you left, I haven't been able to write a single word. Because you were Papa's entire story.

But now, an Auto Memories Doll has given Papa the strength to pick up the pen once more.

I will never forget you. Papa will finish your story.

With love, forever,
Papa
