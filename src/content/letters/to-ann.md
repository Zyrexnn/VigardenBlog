Dearest Ann,

Happy birthday. Mama is sorry she cannot be there with you.

On the day you were born, Mama was the happiest person in the world. When your tiny hand gripped Mama's finger, my life found its meaning.

Every year, I have arranged for this letter to arrive on your birthday. Whether you are laughing or crying, Mama is always by your side.

When you grow up, please remember Mama's words. Mama will love you forever and always.

Always by your side,
Mama
