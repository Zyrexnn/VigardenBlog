Dear Night Sky,

I have spent my life studying the stars. My father and mother both dedicated their lives to the heavens. And I, too, walk the same path.

But since meeting that Auto Memories Doll, I realized something. Simply gazing at the stars is not enough to see what truly matters.

She said, "I am a doll that writes letters. I help turn your feelings into words."

What a strange girl. She claimed not to understand emotions, yet she looked into people's hearts more directly than anyone.

Will we meet again someday? I hope the stars will guide the way.

Under the starlit sky,
Leon Stephanotis
